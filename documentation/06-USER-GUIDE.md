## Getting Started

### System Requirements

**Browser Requirements**:
- Google Chrome (recommended) - Version 90+
- Mozilla Firefox - Version 88+
- Microsoft Edge - Version 90+
- Safari - Version 14+

**Internet Connection**:
- Minimum: 1 Mbps
- Recommended: 5 Mbps or higher

### Accessing the System

1. Open your web browser
2. Navigate to: `https://your-hrms-domain.com`
3. You will see the login page

## Login & Authentication

### First Time Login

1. **Receive Credentials**:
   - You will receive an email with your login credentials
   - Email: Your company email address
   - Temporary Password: Provided by HR

2. **Login Process**:
   - Enter your email address
   - Enter your password
   - Click "Login" button

3. **Change Password** (Recommended):
   - After first login, go to Settings
   - Click "Change Password"
   - Enter current password
   - Enter new password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Confirm new password
   - Click "Update Password"

### Regular Login

1. Go to the HRMS login page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Login"
4. You will be redirected to your dashboard based on your role

### Forgot Password

1. Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Click "Send Reset Link"
4. Check your email for password reset instructions
5. Click the link in the email
6. Enter your new password
7. Confirm the password
8. Click "Reset Password"

### Logout

1. Click on your profile icon (top right corner)
2. Select "Logout" from the dropdown menu
3. You will be redirected to the login page

## Employee Functions

### 1. Mark Attendance

#### Clock In

1. Go to Dashboard
2. Click "Mark Attendance" or "Clock In"
3. System will record your login time
4. You will see confirmation message

**Note**: You can only clock in once per day

#### Clock Out

1. Go to Dashboard
2. Click "Clock Out"
3. Select reason for logout (if prompted)
4. System will calculate your working hours
5. You will see confirmation with total hours worked

#### Break Management

**Start Break**:
1. Click "Start Break"
2. Select break type (Morning/Lunch/Evening)
3. Break timer starts

**End Break**:
1. Click "End Break"
2. Break duration is recorded
3. Break time is deducted from working hours

### 2. Apply for Leave

1. Go to "Leaves" section
2. Click "Apply Leave"
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Leave Type**: Select (Paid Leave, Sick Leave, Casual Leave)
   - **Start Date**: Select from calendar
   - **End Date**: Select from calendar
   - **Reason**: Enter reason for leave (minimum 10 characters)
4. Click "Submit Application"
5. You will receive confirmation
6. Wait for manager and HR approval

**Leave Balance**:
- View your remaining leave balance on the dashboard
- Paid Leave: Updated annually
- Sick Leave: As per company policy

### 3. View Attendance History

1. Go to "Attendance" section
2. Select date range:
   - This Month
   - Last Month
   - Custom Range
3. View your attendance records:
   - Date
   - Clock In time
   - Clock Out time
   - Total hours
   - Status (Present/Absent/Leave)

### 4. View Salary Slip

1. Go to "Payroll" section
2. Click "Salary Slips"
3. Select month and year
4. Click "View Slip"
5. Your salary slip will be displayed
6. Click "Download PDF" to save

**Salary Slip Contains**:
- Basic salary
- Allowances (HRA, Other)
- Deductions (PF, ESI, Tax)
- Net salary
- Bank details

### 5. Update Personal Information

1. Go to "Profile" or "My Account"
2. Click "Edit Profile"
3. Update allowed fields:
   - Contact number
   - Address
   - Emergency contact
4. Click "Save Changes"

**Note**: Some fields can only be updated by HR

### 6. Raise Issue/Complaint

1. Go to "Issues" or "Support"
2. Click "Raise New Issue"
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Issue Type**: Select category
   - **Subject**: Brief description
   - **Details**: Detailed explanation
4. Click "Submit"
5. You will receive a ticket number
6. Track issue status in "My Issues"

### 7. View Announcements

1. Go to Dashboard
2. Check "Announcements" section
3. Click on announcement to read full details
4. Important announcements are highlighted

## HR Functions

### 1. Employee Management

#### Add New Employee

1. Go to "Employees" section
2. Click "Add Employee"
3. Fill in employee details:
   - **Personal Information**: Name, DOB, Gender, Contact
   - **Employment Details**: Emp Code, Department, Role, Joining Date
   - **Salary Information**: CTC, Basic, HRA, Allowances
   - **Bank Details**: Bank Name, Account Number, IFSC
4. Click "Save Employee"
5. System will send credentials to employee's email

#### Edit Employee

1. Go to "Employees" section
2. Search for employee
3. Click "Edit" icon
4. Update required fields
5. Click "Save Changes"

#### View Employee Details

1. Go to "Employees" section
2. Click on employee name
3. View complete profile:
   - Personal information
   - Employment history
   - Attendance summary
   - Leave history
   - Salary details
   - Documents

### 2. Attendance Management

#### View Team Attendance

1. Go to "Attendance" section
2. Select date or date range
3. View attendance report:
   - Employee-wise attendance
   - Present/Absent/Leave count
   - Late arrivals
   - Early departures

#### Approve Attendance Regularization

1. Go to "Attendance" → "Pending Requests"
2. Review regularization request
3. Check reason and supporting documents
4. Click "Approve" or "Reject"
5. Add comments if rejecting

### 3. Leave Management

#### View Leave Applications

1. Go to "Leaves" section
2. View all pending leave applications
3. Filter by:
   - Department
   - Status (Pending/Approved/Rejected)
   - Date range

#### Approve/Reject Leave

1. Click on leave application
2. Review details:
   - Employee name
   - Leave type
   - Dates
   - Reason
   - Leave balance
3. Click "Approve" or "Reject"
4. If rejecting, provide reason
5. Employee will be notified via email

### 4. Payroll Processing

#### Generate Salary Slips

1. Go to "Payroll" section
2. Click "Generate Salary Slips"
3. Select month and year
4. Select department (or All)
5. Click "Generate"
6. System will create salary slips for all employees
7. Review and approve

#### Process Payroll

1. Go to "Payroll" → "Process Payroll"
2. Select month and year
3. Review summary:
   - Total employees
   - Total amount
   - Department-wise breakdown
4. Click "Process Payroll"
5. System will mark payroll as processed

### 5. Reports

#### Attendance Report

1. Go to "Reports" → "Attendance"
2. Select parameters:
   - Date range
   - Department
   - Employee (optional)
3. Click "Generate Report"
4. View report or download as Excel/PDF

#### Leave Report

1. Go to "Reports" → "Leaves"
2. Select parameters
3. Generate report showing:
   - Leave applications
   - Approved/Rejected count
   - Leave balance summary

#### Payroll Report

1. Go to "Reports" → "Payroll"
2. Select month and year
3. Generate report showing:
   - Employee-wise salary
   - Deductions
   - Net pay
   - Bank transfer details

## Admin Functions

### 1. User Management

#### Create User Account

1. Go to "Admin" → "Users"
2. Click "Create User"
3. Fill in details
4. Assign role (Admin/HR/User)
5. Set permissions
6. Click "Create"

#### Manage Roles & Permissions

1. Go to "Admin" → "Roles"
2. View existing roles
3. Click "Edit" to modify permissions
4. Save changes

### 2. System Settings

#### Company Settings

1. Go to "Admin" → "Settings" → "Company"
2. Update:
   - Company name
   - Address
   - Contact details
   - Logo
3. Save changes

#### Attendance Settings

1. Go to "Admin" → "Settings" → "Attendance"
2. Configure:
   - Shift timings
   - Grace period
   - Half-day hours
   - Break durations
3. Save settings

#### Leave Settings

1. Go to "Admin" → "Settings" → "Leaves"
2. Configure:
   - Leave types
   - Annual leave quota
   - Carry forward rules
   - Approval workflow
3. Save settings

#### Payroll Settings

1. Go to "Admin" → "Settings" → "Payroll"
2. Configure:
   - PF percentage
   - ESI limits
   - Professional tax slabs
   - Tax regime settings
3. Save settings

### 3. Department Management

1. Go to "Admin" → "Departments"
2. View all departments
3. Add new department:
   - Department name
   - Department head
   - Description
4. Edit or delete existing departments

### 4. Backup & Restore

#### Create Backup

1. Go to "Admin" → "Backup"
2. Click "Create Backup"
3. Select backup type:
   - Full backup
   - Database only
   - Files only
4. Click "Start Backup"
5. Download backup file when ready

#### Restore Backup

1. Go to "Admin" → "Backup"
2. Click "Restore"
3. Upload backup file
4. Confirm restoration
5. Wait for process to complete

## Troubleshooting

### Cannot Login

**Problem**: "Invalid credentials" error

**Solutions**:
1. Verify email address is correct
2. Check if Caps Lock is on
3. Try "Forgot Password" to reset
4. Contact HR if issue persists

---

### Attendance Not Recorded

**Problem**: Clock in/out not working

**Solutions**:
1. Check internet connection
2. Refresh the page
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try different browser
5. Contact IT support

---

### Leave Application Not Submitting

**Problem**: Error when submitting leave

**Solutions**:
1. Check all required fields are filled
2. Verify dates are correct (end date after start date)
3. Check if you have sufficient leave balance
4. Try again after some time
5. Contact HR if issue persists

---

### Salary Slip Not Visible

**Problem**: Cannot view salary slip

**Solutions**:
1. Check if payroll has been processed for that month
2. Verify you selected correct month/year
3. Clear browser cache
4. Contact HR department

---

### Slow Performance

**Problem**: System is slow

**Solutions**:
1. Check internet speed
2. Close unnecessary browser tabs
3. Clear browser cache and cookies
4. Try different browser
5. Contact IT support if issue persists

---

### Browser Compatibility Issues

**Problem**: Features not working properly

**Solutions**:
1. Update browser to latest version
2. Enable JavaScript
3. Disable browser extensions temporarily
4. Try recommended browser (Chrome)

## Best Practices

### Security

1. **Never share your password** with anyone
2. **Logout** when leaving your desk
3. **Change password** regularly (every 90 days)
4. **Use strong passwords** (mix of letters, numbers, symbols)
5. **Report suspicious activity** immediately

### Attendance

1. **Clock in on time** every day
2. **Remember to clock out** before leaving
3. **Mark breaks** accurately
4. **Apply for regularization** if you forget to clock in/out

### Leave Management

1. **Apply for leave in advance** (at least 3 days)
2. **Provide valid reason** for leave
3. **Check leave balance** before applying
4. **Plan leaves** around project deadlines

### Communication

1. **Check announcements** daily
2. **Respond to notifications** promptly
3. **Update contact information** if changed
4. **Raise issues** through proper channels
